Frequently Asked Questions

GolijaTrail 2024 is an annual trail running event held in the Golija mountains, offering different race categories and scenic routes.

You can register for GolijaTrail 2024 by filling out the registration form available on the "Register" section of our website.

Participants should bring appropriate trail running gear, including comfortable shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and any personal hydration or nutrition supplies.
