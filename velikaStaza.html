Velika staza - 20km

Experience the beauty of Golija through a unique and challenging trail run.

Course Overview

The GolijaTrail 2024 running course spans a total distance of 50 kilometers, starting and ending at Odvraćenica. The course takes you through diverse terrains including dense forests, open meadows, and rocky paths, offering breathtaking views of the Golija mountain range.

Key Features:

  • Distance: 50 kilometers
  • Elevation Gain: 2000 meters
  • Start/Finish: Odvraćenica
  • Maximum Elevation: 1833 meters

Course Map

Checkpoints

The course includes several checkpoints where runners can refuel, get medical assistance, and check their progress. Below are the main checkpoints:

  • Checkpoint 1: 10 km mark - Refreshments and medical aid available.
  • Checkpoint 2: 20 km mark - Refreshments, snacks, and medical aid available.
  • Checkpoint 3: 30 km mark - Refreshments, energy gels, and medical aid available.
  • Checkpoint 4: 40 km mark - Refreshments, snacks, and medical aid available.

Height Profile

The height profile of the GolijaTrail 2024 course shows the elevation changes runners will experience. The course features several challenging ascents and descents, adding to the excitement and challenge of the race.

Course Rules

  • All participants must wear their race bibs at all times.
  • Littering on the course is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants must follow the marked trail at all times.
  • Any form of external assistance is not allowed.
  • Participants must check in at all checkpoints.

Safety Measures

The safety of our participants is our top priority. The following safety measures will be in place:

  • Medical teams will be stationed at each checkpoint.
  • Emergency response teams will be on standby throughout the course.
  • Participants are required to carry a mobile phone with them.
  • Weather conditions will be monitored continuously, and participants will be informed of any changes.
